

Vodafone Qatar has announced that it will offer the iPhone 6s and iPhone 6s Plus
from October 10.
In this connection, Vodafone will have a launch event at Villaggio, which will
start at 11pm tomorrow and include entertainment and giveaways. Customers
joining Vodafone at the launch will need to access Villaggio through Gate 4 and
be able to pick up or buy their device at 12.01am on Saturday, October 10.
In a statement yesterday, Vodafone Qatar said iPhone 6s and iPhone 6s Plus
customers could take "full advantage" of the company's 4G LTE network as well as
the selection of prepaid and postpaid options.
"Our customers have been eagerly anticipating the arrival of the new iPhones in
Qatar since the announcement in early September," said Kyle Whitehill, CEO,
Vodafone Qatar. "We're thrilled to bring these innovative phones to customers
through our wide variety of offers, ensuring that everyone has a plan that works
best for them."
Customer can buy the iPhone 6s and iPhone 6s Plus at Vodafone shops throughout
Qatar and online at www.vodafone.qa
