

Bigwig retailer Best Buy is, for a limited period, offering Apple's newest
iPhone 6s handset just for $1, to customers who sign-up for a two-year contract
with Verizon or Sprint.

Announcing the limited-time promotion of the iPhone 6s for $1, Best Buy said
that that the model being offered for the sale is the 16GB variant of the iPhone
6S handset. The regular retail cost of the handset is $200 with a two-year
contract. The promotion is applicable to the iPhone 6s handset in all four color
options --- Silver, Gold, Space Gray, and Rose Gold.

The $1 iPhone 6s sale announced by Best Buy will end on Sunday, December 20,
2015. The sale price can be availed by customers both online -- at the
BestBuy.com website -- and in person at a Best Buy store.

In fact, Best Buy is also offering the $1 deal -- with two-year contract -- for
the 16GB variant of the iPhone 6 model as well. The normal cost of the handset
at retail stores is $100. The iPhone 6 versions being sold by Best Buy for $1
are the 'Space Gray' and 'Silver' models of the handset.

Moreover, along with the $1 promotion price for the mentioned iPhone 6s and
iPhone 6 models, Best Buy is also offering a $200 gift card to customers who
trade in their iPhone 5 handsets in good working condition.
