

NEW DELHI: Vodafone India, the country's second largest telecom service
provider, Friday said that it will offer iPhone 6s and iPhone 6s Plus, beginning
on Friday, October 16, 2015. The company has also announced its data and calling
plan for Apple iPhones with benefits worth Rs 8,885.

Existing or new Vodafone India customers can buy a new iPhone 6s or iPhone 6s
Plus and get a fully built Vodafone RED 1299 worry-free plan free for six months
from the time of purchase.

The Vodafone RED 1299 monthly plan includes 3GB mobile internet quota, 4000 free
Local and STD minutes and 1500 local and national SMS.

Charges above plan limits will be 50p/MB for data usage, 50p/SMS and 50p/min for
voice calls. The company said that roaming, International calling and VAS
charges will be extra as applicable.

Vodafone said that all customers on the RED 1299 plan will get access to a
relationship manager and get priority access at the Vodafone stores. In
addition, customers can add family members and devices. The usage can be pooled
and shared among them.

Vodafone customers can pre-order the new iPhone 6s and iPhone 6s Plus starting
today on www.vodafone.in or Vodafone's official onoine partner Amazon.
