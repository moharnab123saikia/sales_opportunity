
DATELINE: KUALA LUMPUR


KUALA LUMPUR, Oct. 15 -- IPhone 6s and iPhone 6s Plus will now be available with
Celcom Axiata Bhd and Digi Telecommunications Sdn Bhd from tonight. The iPhone
6s 16GB of Celcom will be available for RM1,058 from Oct. 16 morning, whereas,
iPhone 6s Plus 16GB will be available at RM1,478.

In addition, the customers will also get a limited period offer of12GB
complimentary data (1GB per month for 12 months). Besides that, iPhone 6, iPhone
6 Plus and iPhone 5s are also available.

According to Digi, an extra RM200 rebate will be offered to customers who
pre-order an iPhone on signing up with a Digi SmartPlan 98. In addition, the
customers who change over to Digi will be saving RM300.

The four main outlets where Celcom customers can purchase iPhone 6s and iPhone
6s Plus are Blue Cube@Sunway Pyramid, Blue Cube Publika, Celcom Centre Taman
Molek, Johor Baru and Blue Cube Elit Avenue Bayan Baru, Penang. Apart from that,
Celcom will also be offering trade-in values for current iPhones.

However, Digi will be conducting various launch events at six Digi Stores around
the country. The stores include SS2, Petaling Jaya; Kuantan, Pahang; Pulau
Tikus, Penang; Danga Bay, Johor; Kuching, Sarawak and Api Api, Sabah. The
customers will be available to make their purchases of the iPhone from these
stores.
