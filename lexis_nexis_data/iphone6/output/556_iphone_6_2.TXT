Rumor: iPhone 6 to have a bigger screen



LENGTH: 136  words





Apple's next flagship phone may come with a considerably larger display than

before.The post Rumor: iPhone 6 to have a bigger screen appeared first on

tbreak.



And so churns the iPhone 6 rumor mill. According to analyst Timothy Arcuri, of

Cowen and Company, Apple's next flagship iPhone may have a bigger screen.



Their sources claim Apple may opt for a 4.8-inch for the iPhone 6, which is a

considerable upgrade from iPhone 5S' 4-inch display. The smartphone will also

feature 802.11ac Wi-Fi functionality, according to the sources.



If history is any indicator, Apple is all but gearing up to announce and release

the next iPhone in the coming months.
