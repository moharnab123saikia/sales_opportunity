Apple iPhone 6 Gets A Price Cut; Now It Costs Just INR 33,990



BYLINE: Phalgunn Maharishi



LENGTH: 250  words





Users can also avail up to INR 22,000 off on exchange program, along with INR

3,000 cashback offer for Citi Credit Card holders.



In just over a few weeks since the launch of the new iPhone 7 and iPhone 7 Plus

in India, Flipkart is now offering a pretty interesting discount on the price

tag of Apple iPhone 6. Users can now purchase an iPhone 6 at a price of INR

33,990 instead of the INR 36,990 price tag, thereby giving a discount of INR

3,000 to the new buyers.



Adding further, the e-commerce portal has also offered upto INR 22,000 off on

exchange, and additionally, customers can avail a discount of 5 per cent by

using an Axis Bank Buzz Credit Cards. Also, Citi Credit card holders can get INR

3,000 cash back.



To refresh your minds, Apple had slashed the price tags of iPhone 6s and iPhone

6s Plus for upto INR 22,000 last month. This means that the iPhone 6s (128 GB

variant) is now available for INR 60,000 instead of the previous INR 82,000

price tag.



Along with this, iPhone SE (64 GB variant) has also received a price slash of

INR 5,000 giving it a price tag of INR 44,000 instead of the previous INR

49,000.



Speaking about the iPhone 6 specifications, you get an 8 MP primary camera with

LED flash, and 1.2 MP front facing camera, along with a fingerprint sensor, 4G

LTE connectivity support, 1 GB RAM, and 16 GB of internal storage. As far as

display is concerned, the iPhone 6 comes with a 4.7 inch Retina HD LE backlit

touchscreen while being powered by a quad core Apple A8 mobile chipset.
