iPhone 6, 6 Plus at discounted rate



LENGTH: 101 words



DATELINE: KATHMANDU





KATHMANDU, March 17 -- Oliz Store has announced attractive offers for Apple's

iPhone 6 and 6 Plus. Issuing a statement, Oliz Store said that iPhone 6 (64GB)

is available at Rs 78,000 and iPhone 6 (128GB) at Rs 88,000.



Similarly, Oliz Store has also offered iPhone 6 Plus (16GB) at Rs 78,000. While

iPhone 6 is available in gold, space gray and silver, iPhone 6 Plus is available

in silver color at the store, it added.
