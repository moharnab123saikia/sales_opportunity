Apple Inc sells record 10 million in first three days



LENGTH: 213 words



DATELINE: India





India, Sept. 23 -- Tech giant Apple on Monday said that it has set a new record

of selling over 10 million of its new iPhone 6 and iPhone 6 Plus models, just

three days after the launch on September 19.



The US-based company said that iPhone 6 and iPhone 6 Plus are available in the

US, Australia, Canada, France, Germany, Hong Kong, Japan, Puerto Rico, Singapore

and the UK and will be available in more than 20 additional countries on

September 26.



Commenting on the development, Apple, CEO, Tim Cook said, "Sales for iPhone 6

and iPhone 6 Plus exceeded our expectations for the launch weekend, and we

couldn't be happier. We would like to thank all of our customers for making this

our best launch ever, shattering all previous sell-through records by a large

margin."
