iPhone 6 and 6 plus discount



LENGTH: 66  words





Oliz Store has announced attractive offers for Apple's iPhone 6 and 6 Plus.

Issuing a statement, Oliz Store said that iPhone 6 (64GB) is available at Rs

78,000 and iPhone 6 (128GB) at Rs 88,000.



Similarly, Oliz Store has also offered iPhone 6 Plus (16GB) at Rs 78,000. While

iPhone 6 is available in gold, space gray and silver, iPhone 6 Plus is available

in silver colour at the store, it added.
