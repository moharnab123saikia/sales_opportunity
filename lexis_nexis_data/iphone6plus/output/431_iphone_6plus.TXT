Apple's Diwali bonanza: iPhone 6 to hit stores in India on Oct 17



LENGTH: 236 words



DATELINE: India





India, Sept. 10 -- The much-anticipated Apple's smartphones iPhone 6 and iPhone

6 Plus will arrive in India this festival season to take on challengers like LG

G3, HTC One and Galaxy Note 4.



Apple will launch its new smartphones, the iPhone 6 and iPhone 6 Plus alongside

the smart watch in India from October 17, according to the company's India

website.



Meanwhile, the smartphone giant will release iPhone 6 to selected countries on

September 19 which includes Australia, Canada, France, Germany, Hong Kong,

Japan, Puerto Rico, Singapore and the UK.



On Tuesday, Apple Inc has launched two new sizes of iPhone and as per rumors,

the new models come in 4.7- and 5.5-inch versions. The new devices are known as

the iPhone 6 and iPhone 6 Plus, respectively.



But the biggest announcement has been the launch of Apple Watch, the Company's

first foray into the world of wearable technology. It boasts of features like

inductive charging, sapphire glass, swappable bands, a heartbeat sensor and a

rotating digital crown.



The watch will come in two different sizes and three different collections:

Watch, Watch Sport and Watch Edition. Prices start at USD 349 and the Apple

Watch will be available "early next year."
