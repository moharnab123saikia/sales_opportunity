Airtel to Offer Latest iPhones from Friday



SECTION: BUSINESS OF BRANDS



LENGTH: 268 words





Bharti Airtel will offer iPhone 6s and iPhone 6s Plus, the latest to come out

from the Apple stable, beginning Friday , October 16 through 235 of its retail

stores across the country . Fifty seven of Bharti Airtel's retail stores will

stay open all night so customers can be among the first in India to get their

hands on the all-new iPhone 6s and iPhone 6s Plus.



Customers can sign up for the iPhone Infinity plans, which include 60GB of free

4G data over 12 months, saving customers ' . 15,000 on their monthly bill, the

company said in a statement on Tuesday .



"With the rise of 4G services, we expect new iPhone customers to enjoy a

worldclass experience on Bharti Airtel," said Srini Gopalan, director, consumer

business, Bharti Airtel. ET had reported the development last week.



Earlier, Vodafone India had announced data and calling plans for iPhone 6s and

iPhone 6s Plus with benefits. The Vodafone's '. 1299 monthly plan includes 3GB

mobile internet quota, 4000 free Local



and STD minutes and 1500 local and national SMS, for six months. Charges above

plan limits will be 50 paisa/MB for data usage, 50 paisa/message and 50

paisa/minute for voice calls. On Tuesday, mobile wallet Mobikwik said its

release of the iOS app -version 5.6 launched on



October 11 -with its presses, peeks, and pops, will support the 3D Force Touch

feature and iOS 9 users in India.



Following the update to the MobiKwik app for iOS, this enhancement will be

exploited to let users recharge their devices, transfer money, and shop online

more quickly and directly from homescreen of their iPhone 6S and 6S Plus using

Quick Actions.
