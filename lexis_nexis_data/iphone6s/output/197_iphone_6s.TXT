Airtel to offer new iPhones 6s and 6s plus from Friday



SECTION: HARDWARE



LENGTH: 201 words





NEW DELHI: Bharti Airtel will offer iPhone 6s and iPhone 6s Plus, the latest to

come out from the Apple stable, beginning Friday, October 16 through 235 of its

retail stores across the country. Fifty seven of Bharti Airtel's retail stores

will stay open all night so customers can be among the first in India to get

their hands on the all-new iPhone 6s and iPhone 6s Plus.



Customers can sign up for the iPhone Infinity plans, which include 60GB of free

4G data over 12 months, saving customers Rs 15,000 on their monthly bill, the

company said in a statement on Tuesday.



"With the rise of 4G services, we expect new iPhone customers to enjoy a

world-class experience on Bharti Airtel," said Srini Gopalan, director -

consumer business, Bharti Airtel. ET had reported the development last week.



Earlier, Vodafone India had announced data and calling plans for iPhone 6s and

iPhone 6s Plus with benefits. The Vodafone's Rs 1,299 monthly plan includes 3GB

mobile internet quota, 4,000 free Local and STD minutes and 1,500 local and

national SMS, for six months.



Charges above plan limits will be 50 paise/MB for data usage, 50 paise/message

and 50 paise/minute for voice calls.
