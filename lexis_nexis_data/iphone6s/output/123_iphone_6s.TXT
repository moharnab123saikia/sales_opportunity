

By tbreak Staff -

Apple fans in Dubai can finally get their hands on the iPhone 6s and its Plus
variant, as it is officially available for sale since 10th October.

Du had announced that it will offer the latest iPhone 6s and 6s Plus, from
Saturday onwards. The iPhone 6s 16GB and iPhone 6s Plus 16GB will be available
from du for a low monthly fee of AED300, which also includes zero upfront fees,
11GB of data and 300 flexible minutes.

Fahad Alhassawi, Chief Commercial Officer for du said that "With our attractive
plans and unbeatable 4G LTE network, iPhone customers will have a seamless
experience with du."

The iPhone 6s and 6s Plus brings new elements with revolutionary Multi Touch
interface and 3D Touch, which senses how deeply you press the button. The latest
iPhones also have Live Photos, bring images to life, which are taken with the
12MP iSight camera and the 5MP HD camera. It is powered by the Apple A9 chip and
built with a 7000 series aluminium for a strong metallic finish.

Customers can buy the iPhone 6s and 6s Plus from the du shops or from the du
website at www.du.ae where they can also avail lots of offers.
