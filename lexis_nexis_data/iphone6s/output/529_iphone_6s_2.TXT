THE OPTIONS



SECTION: NEWS; NATIONAL; Pg. 16



LENGTH: 220 words





New Zealand mobile operators Spark, Vodafone and 2degrees are all offering the

new devices with a range of price plans with various limits and payment options.



Here is a selection.



VODAFONE



iPhone 6S



The 16GB model is available for free on the Vodafone Red+ plan for $129 per

month on a 24-month term and you get unlimited minutes and 7.5GB of data.



iPhone 6S Plus



The 16GB version will cost $199 on the Vodafone Red+ plan for $129 per month on

a 24-month term.



The cheapest plan costs $39 a month, with the 16GB phone costing $1299.



SPARK



iPhone 6S



The 16GB model is available for $199 on a $140-a-month plan for a 24-month term.

This drops down to $100 a month (unlimited calls and 10GB of data) but the phone

costs $599.



iPhone 6S Plus



The larger iPhone costs $140 a month for the 16GB model with a upfront cost of

$399. That includes unlimited calls and 10GB of data.



2DEGREES



iPhone 6S



The cheapest plan is $39 a month (400 minutes, 1.5GB data) and you pay off the

phone at $49 a month interest free. The most expensive is $89 a month (unlimited

minutes, 8GB data)



iPhone 6S Plus



It works in a similar way for the larger phone except your monthly payment for

the phone is $58 on top of the monthly plan.
