iPhone 6s, 6s plus to be launched on Nov 13



LENGTH: 202  words



DATELINE: KARACHI





KARACHI, Nov. 11 -- Apple iPhone 6s and 6s plus are coming to Pakistan on

November 13 with its official distributor, Future Tech.



Sale of the most advanced iPhone would commence on Friday, Nov 13 at Advance

Telecom retail outlets across Pakistan. Future Tech is planning to hold events

in three cities, at Dolmen Mall in Karachi, Fortress Stadium in Lahore and

Centaurus Mall in Islamabad. The distributor expects big turnover at the venues.



iPhone lovers are excited over the news that they would have an official Apple

presence in Pakistan, where sales and after sales services would also be

available. In addition to this, Future Tech is providing incentives to the first

500 customers on pre-booking and purchase through its 'buy one get one'

promotion.



The buyers would now have the official Apple warranty available in Pakistan

also, which was not



there before.



Apple iPhone 6s and 6s plus are now on sale via online pre-orders. Pre-book your

iPhone by logging on to the booking form available at Future Tech website.
