
DATELINE: New Delhi


New Delhi, Sept. 10 -- Apple has unveiled its updated smartphones, the iPhone 6S
and iPhone 6S Plus, giving its large-screen handsets a new pressure-sensitive
display with '3D Touch' gestures.
 The standout feature of the new iPhone 6S and 6S Plus is the new touchscreen
technology called '3D Touch'.
 The pressure-sensitive touchscreen can detect various types of tap. From a
single traditional tap to heavier presses, each gesture can produce a different
interaction with the smartphone.
