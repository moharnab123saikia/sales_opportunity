Bianca Mosca Adds Bespoke Alligator Cases for iPhone 6s



LENGTH: 189  words





Oct. 20 -- Bianca Mosca known for it's ultra-luxury exotic skin bags, wallets

and accessories has launched a range of hand crafted cases specially designed

for the new Apple iPhone 6s made from prime Louisiana alligator leather in

fifteen vibrant colors.



The new cases can be personalized with a customer's initials and come in colors

such as Blue Jeans, Lipstick Red, Distressed Beige as well as traditional Black

and Brown.



A Bianca Mosca spokesperson said: "It's a slim case that fit's easily to your

phone and is simple to remove if required. All ports and features of the phone

remain easily accessible. This is the truly luxurious way to protect and secure

your precious Apple iPhone."



The cases are made entirely by hand in the company's workshop in France and are

designed to fit all iPhone 6 and 6s models. There are two variants: one to fit

the standard 6 and 6s models and a larger size for the 6 Plus and 6s Plus. The

cases cost $395 including up to four initials and delivery within the

continental USA.



For more information or to order online visit the Bianca Mosca E-Shop at:

https://www.biancamosca.com.
