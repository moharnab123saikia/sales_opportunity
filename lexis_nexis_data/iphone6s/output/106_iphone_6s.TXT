Globe to offer iPhone 6s and iPhone 6s Plus



SECTION: Pg. S2/8



LENGTH: 130 words





GLOBE TELECOM announced it will offer iPhone 6s and iPhone 6s Plus, the most

advanced iPhones ever, beginning on Nov. 6.



iPhone 6s 16GB will be available on Plan 1799 with P450 monthly cashout. iPhone

6s Plus 16GB will also be available on Plan 1799 with P650 monthly cashout.

iPhone 6, iPhone 6 Plus and iPhone 5s will also be available.



iPhone 6s and iPhone 6s Plus bring a powerful new dimension to iPhone's

revolutionary Multi-Touch interface with 3D Touch, which senses force to enable

intuitive new ways to access features and interact with content.



On Plan 1799, customers can enjoy a best-value postpaid plan package with bigger

data allocation and free access to exclusive content for a complete digital

experience.



For more information on iPhone, visit www.apple.com/iphone.
