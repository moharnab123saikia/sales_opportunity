Apple iPhone 6 receives price cut on Flipkart: Here's how much it costs



BYLINE: DNA Web Team



LENGTH: 209  words





The device is now available on discount!



Flipkart is offering the iPhone 6 at a discounted price. The device (will now be

available at a price of Rs 33,990, down from Rs 36,990 in India.



Those interested can also get up to Rs 22,000 off on exchange. Additionally, you

can get an extra Rs 2,000 off over and above Regular Exchange Value. You can

avail 5 percent off on Axis Bank Buzz Credit Cards and also, flat Rs 3000

cashback on Citi Credit cards.



Last month, Apple slashed prices for the iPhone 6s and iPhone 6s Plus in India

by up to Rs 22,000. The iPhone 6s (128GB) which was earlier priced at Rs 82,000

will now be available for Rs 60,000. It has received a price cut of Rs 22,000.

It's larger sibling, the iPhone 6s Plus (128GB), which was earlier priced at Rs

92,000 will now be available at Rs 70,000. It has received a price cut of Rs

22,000 as well.



The iPhone SE (64GB) which was earlier priced at Rs 49,000 will now be available

for Rs 44,000 in India. It has received a price cut of Rs 5000.



Recently, Apple introduces the iPhone 7 and iPhone 7 Plus with features like a

new camera system, better battery life, and stereo speakers, two new finishes,

and is the first water and dust resistant iPhone.
