New year bonanza for Apple fans! iPhone 6s, 6s Plus get massive price cut



LENGTH: 149  words



DATELINE: New Delhi





New Delhi, Dec. 21 -- Zee Media Bureau

 Good news for all iPhone lovers! Apple has reduced the price of iPhone 6s and

iPhone 6s Plus in India by upto 16 percent.





 As per a report in Times of India, the 6s and iPhone 6s Plus will get reduction

in prices between 11 and 16 percent. Apple has made this move to rake up sales

in the country.

 Within just two months of launching, the base model iPhone 6s which was

launched at Rs 62,000 will now cost between Rs 52,000 and Rs 55,000.

 All the variants of the latest iPhones -16 GB, 64 GB and 128 GB are getting the

price cuts, the TOI reported.

 It may also be recalled that, the iPhone maker just few days ago slashed the

price of iPhone 5s by almost half.

 The 6S has the 4.7-inch (about 12-centimeter) display of its predecessor and

the 6S Plus - which updates one of the more popular handsets in the "phablet

category" - has the same 5.5-inch screen.
