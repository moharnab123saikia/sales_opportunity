Comparison: Motorola Moto X Force with Motorola Moto X Play and Apple iPhone 6s



SECTION: MOBILES



LENGTH: 291 words





NEW DELHI: Motorola finally launched its much awaited 'shatterproof'

smartphonein India. The handset is the talk of the town because of its powerful

specifications and the shatterproof shield technology. We compared the Motorola

Moto X Force withand, so that you can choose the one right for you.



Display



Motorola Moto X Force: 5.4-inch



Motorola Moto X Play: 5.5-inch



Apple iPhone 6s: 4.7-inch



Operating System



Motorola Moto X Force: Android 5.1.1 Lollipop (upgradeable to Marshmallow)



Motorola Moto X Play: Android 5.1.1 Lollipop



Apple iPhone 6s: iOS 9 (upgradeable to iOS9.2)



Processor



Motorola Moto X Force: Quad-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 615 processor



Motorola Moto X Play: Quad-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 810 processor



Apple iPhone 6s: 1.84GHz Dual-core processor



Storage



Motorola Moto X Force: 32GB/64GB



Motorola Moto X Play: 16GB/32GB



Apple iPhone 6s: 16GB/64GB/128GB



RAM



Motorola Moto X Force: 3GB



Motorola Moto X Play: 2GB



Apple iPhone 6s: 2GB



Camera



Motorola Moto X Force: 21 MP, f/2.0, phase detection autofocus, dual-LED (dual

tone) flash



Motorola Moto X Play: 21 MP, f/2.0, phase detection autofocus, dual-LED (dual

tone) flash



Apple iPhone 6s: 12 MP, f/2.2, 29mm, phase detection autofocus, dual-LED (dual

tone) flash



Connectivity



Motorola Moto X Force: Wi-Fi 802.11 a/b/g/n/ac, dual-band, hotspot, Bluetooth,

GPS, NFC, Infrared



Motorola Moto X Play: Wi-Fi 802.11 a/b/g/n, dual-band, hotspot, Bluetooth, GPS,

NFC



Apple iPhone 6s: Wi-Fi 802.11 a/b/g/n/ac, dual-band, hotspot, Bluetooth, NFC,

GPS



Battery



Motorola Moto X Force: 3,760 mAh



Motorola Moto X Play: 3,630 mAh



Apple iPhone 6s: 1,715 mAh



Price



Motorola Moto X Force: starting at Rs 49,999



Motorola Moto X Play: starting at Rs 16,999



Apple iPhone 6s: starting at Rs 45,4999
