Apple iPhone 6s, 6s Plus to be avaiable in India from Oct 16



LENGTH: 105  words



DATELINE: Chennai





Chennai, Oct. 1 -- Apple's iPhone 6s and iPhone 6s Plus will be available in

India from October 16 at Redington India, one of the main distributors of

iPhones.





 The iPhones would be available for purchase across India at its 3,000 retail

outlets, Chennai-based Redington India said in a statement.

 Apple Inc began the sales of iPhone 6s and iPhone 6s Plus in United States and

in other countries recently.

 Redington India, which began operations in 1993, currently has its presence in

India, the Middle East, Africa, Turkey and Sri Lanka.

 Shares of Redington India were trading at Rs 110.30 apiece, down by 0.81 per

cent on the BSE.
