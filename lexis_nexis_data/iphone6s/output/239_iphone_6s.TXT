GP brings iPhone 6s and 6s Plus



LENGTH: 171 words



DATELINE: Bangladesh





Bangladesh, Nov. 7 -- Grameenphone officially launched iPhone 6s and 6s Plus.

Grameenphone is the first operator in Bangladesh to deliver the latest IPhone 6s

and 6s plus to the local consumers. Mr. Yasir Azman, Chief Marketing Officer of

Grameenphone handed over the latest iPhone handsets among the pre booking

customers. The pre booking started on October 23, 2015. GP started delivering

iPhone to pre booking customers from its 17 GPC's and Experience Center across

the country. From now on iPhone 6s and 6s Plus will be available at Grameenphone

Centers. The Star Customers will get 24 EMI, while general customers will get 12

EMI on the handset price. iPhone buyers will also get 50% discount on Heavy

Browsing Nonstop Internet package of GP, which they can buy 8 times in the first

two months of the phone purchase.
