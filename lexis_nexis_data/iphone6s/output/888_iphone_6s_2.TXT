Warid to launch iPhone6s and iPhone6s Plus for its customers



SECTION: Vol. 25 No. 252



LENGTH: 160  words





Warid Telecom, leading Pakistani telecom operator, is officially launching two

coveted iPhones for its valuable subscribers. Warid will offer iPhone 6s and

iPhone 6s Plus, the most advanced iPhones, from Friday, November 13th 2015.

Customers can pre-order iPhone 6s and iPhone 6s Plus at www.waridtel.com/4G.



This new development will give both new and existing Warid customers access to

two of the most popular phones available in the market today. Both devices are

4G enabled which means they will be able to connect to Warid's Lightning Taiz

LTE mobile network. They also boast advanced features such as excellent cameras,

high-speed processors, and large RAMs; all run via Apple's popular iOS.



Warid has always excelled in bringing new and innovative technology to their

consumers. With the introduction of these two highly desired devices Warid is

continuing to ensure that valued customers are satisfied and always have a

plethora of options to choose from.**
