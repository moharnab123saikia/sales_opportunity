iPhone 6s, 6s Plus all set to hit Indian stores on October 16



LENGTH: 204  words



DATELINE: New Delhi





New Delhi, Oct. 15 -- Zee Media Bureau

 This is indeed a piece of good news for all iPhone fans as Apple Inc's latest

handsets, the iPhone 6s and iPhone 6s Plus are all set to hit stores on Friday

(October 16) across the country.





 After three weeks of its official launch in the United States, the new iPhones

will hit the Indian smartphone market in the intervening night of October 15-16,

much to the delight of the fans.

 However, costumers will have to shelf out a heavy sum for the iPhone 6s and

iPhone 6s Plus as they have been priced starting from Rs 62,000 for the lower

variant up to Rs 92,000 for the highest variant.

 Nevertheless, it is interesting to note that India's leading telecom service

providers Airtel and Vodafone are offering a series of freebies up to Rs 15,000

in order to woo customers.

 ALSO READ: Airtel offers freebies worth Rs 15,000 with iPhone 6s, 6s Plus

 The iPhones will be available in three variants of 16GB, 64GB and 128GB.

 The prices of the various variants are given below.

 iPhone 6s price:

 16GB - Rs 62,000/

 64GB - Rs 72,000/

 128GB - Rs 82,000/

 iPhone 6s Plus price:

 16GB - Rs 72,000/

 64GB - Rs 82,000/

 128GB- Rs 92,000/
