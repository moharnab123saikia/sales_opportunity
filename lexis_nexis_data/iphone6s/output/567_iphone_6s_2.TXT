Etisalat announces smart pay plan for iPhone 6s and 6s Plus



LENGTH: 270  words





By tbreak Staff -



Etisalat announced yesterday that customers can get the iPhone 6s and 6s Plus

with a post-paid connection for a smart pay plan starting from AED 115/ month.

The smart pay plans are available on up to 24 months contractual basis.



This first of its kind offer allows Etisalat's customers in the UAE to buy the

latest iPhones as per their convenience and suited to their payment requirements

across 12, 18 or 24 months. Customers can get the iPhone 6s and 6s Plus in all

colors - white, grey, gold and silver, and for all capacities- 16, 64 and 128GB

with the smart pay plan. Depending on the device and payment period, the price

starts with AED 115 per month and can go all the way up to AED 320 per month.



Khaled ElKhouly, Chief Consumer Officer at Etisalat, said, "We are committed to

continuously offer new deals to our customers by partnering with leading global

brands and mobile manufacturers. iPhone is undoubtedly one of the most popular

mobile phones demanded by our customers. Through the new Smart pay options, we

are making it highly affordable for all customer segments to get their hands on

this highly desired smartphone. It lets users enjoy the fast speed 4G experience

that these advanced phones enable on our nation-wide 4G network."



Customers can also enjoy the high internet speeds of up to 300 Mbps thanks to

Etisalat's 4G/LTE network which covers nearly 95 percent of the country's

populated regions. Customers can also download the mobile app on all platforms

to stay updated on the latest services and products offered by Etisalat.
