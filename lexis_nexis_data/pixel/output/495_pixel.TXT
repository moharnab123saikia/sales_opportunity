Motorola Moto G coming to India this month, Google Edition now available



LENGTH: 182  words





The Google Moto G budget stock Android phone has come up for sale now. The

handset has appeared on the Google Play website yesterday.



Since the Motorola's Moto G also comes with the almost the same OS, there is

hardly any difference between the two. The only positive point it will gain will

be in terms of OS update  it will receive. Priced at $179, the unlocked handset

works on either AT&T or T-Mobile's 3G networks, reports Androidguys.



The handset is ecpected to land in India in the last week of January. The

officials of Motorola revealed tis information to TimesofIndia on the sidelines

of Consumer Electronics Show, says the news report.



The Moto G is cheap phone and is the first Motorola phone after Google bought

the company in year 2012. It is a 4.5-inch display phone with Qualcomm

Snapdragon 400 quad-core processor and 1 GB of RAM. The display resolution is of

1280 x 720 pixels that offers a display density of 329 pixels per inch (ppi).

This display density is quite close the iPhone 5S and 5C's display that is 640 x

1136 pixels on a 4-inch display for a 326 ppi pixel density.
