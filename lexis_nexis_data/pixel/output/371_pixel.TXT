
DATELINE: ALEXANDRIA, Va


United States Patent and Trademark Office has granted patent no. 8,866,928 on
October 21, 2014, to Google Inc (California), titled as "Determining exposure
times using split paxels"

Inventors:  Geiss; Ryan (Mountain View, CA)
Assignee:  Google Inc. (Mountain View, CA)

According to the abstract released by the U.S. Patent & Trademark Office: "A
plurality of images of a scene may be captured. Each image of the plurality of
images may be captured using a different total exposure time (TET). The images
in the plurality of images may be downsampled. A pixel value histogram based on
pixel values of the downsampled images may be constructed. The pixel value
histogram may be compared to one or more reference pixel value histograms. A
payload TET may be determined based on comparing the pixel value histogram to
the one or more reference pixel value histograms."

The patent was filed on December 18, 2012 Application no. 13/718,533
