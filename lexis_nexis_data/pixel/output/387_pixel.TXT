
DATELINE: ALEXANDRIA, Va


United States Patent and Trademark Office has granted patent no. 8,611,696 on
December 17, 2013, to Google Inc (California), titled as "Panoramic image fill"

Inventors:  Zhu; Jiajun (Mountain View, CA), Ogale; Abhijit (Mountain View, CA)

Assignee:  Google Inc. (Mountain View, CA)

According to the abstract released by the U.S. Patent & Trademark Office:
"Systems and methods for filling panoramic images having valid and invalid pixel
regions are provided. An invalid region is identified in an initial panoramic
image. Pixel data of invalid pixels in the initial panoramic image are replaced
with pixel data of pixels from a valid region in at least one nearby panoramic
image to obtain a valid fill region."

The patent was filed on April 6, 2012 Application no. 13/441,588
