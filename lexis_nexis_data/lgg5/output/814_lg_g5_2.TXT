Pramerica Real Estate Investors has obtained an extension to a  125m finance

facility that its Central European Fund has with pbb Deutsche Pfandbriefbank.



pbb is sole lender and hedge provider for the refinanced facility, which covers

deals on three CEE properties: the 18,000 m2 Trinity Park office building in

Warsaw; the 15,000 m2 A7 Arena office complex in Prague; and a 69,000 m2 retail

portfolio in Slovenia.
