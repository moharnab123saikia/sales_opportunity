HTC 10 with QHD display, fingerprint sensor launched in India at Rs 52,990



SECTION: MOBILES



LENGTH: 254 words





NEW DELHI: HTC has launched its flagship smartphone HTC 10 in India at a price

tag of Rs 52,990. The metal-clad smartphone will be available starting 5th June

and the first 500 buyers will also get the Ice View HTC 10 cover. The handset

was unveiled globally in April this year.Although the Taiwanese company

introduced a Qualcomm Snapdragon 820 processor variant of HTC 10 in the US, the

company's India website initially listed another handset called the HTC 10

Lifestyle.



The version came with Qualcomm Snapdragon 652 SoC. The company, however, later

confirmed that the Snapdragon 820 version will be coming to India.The HTC 10

sports a 5.2-inch QHD super LCD touchscreen with a resolution of 2560x1440 pixel

and Gorilla Glass protection. It runs Android 6.0 Marshmallow-based Sense UI and

mounts a 12MP UltraPixel f/1.8 camera at the back along with a 5MP A/F selfie

shooter.Boasting a metal unibody design, it supports 4G connectivity and is

powered by HTC BoomSound Hi-Fi Edition with Dolby Audio along with a 3,000 mAh

battery. It includes a USB Type-C port and a fingerprint scanner at the bottom

bezel area.As for the storage, the HTC 10 comes in 32GB and 64GB variants. The

HTC 10 Lifestyle comes in 32GB variant only. Both versions support microSD card

of up to 2TB. The handset will be available in Glacier Silver and Carbon Grey

colour variants.The smartphone will rival other flagship smartphones like the

Samsung Galaxy S7, LG G5 and Sony Xperia Z5 Premium in the Indian market.
